Europe

Europe is the second smallest continent but rich in history and culture.

Countries

It is home to over 740 million people across more than 40 countries.

The European Union (EU) is a major political and economic alliance in Europe

Europe has diverse landscapes, from the Alps to Mediterranean beaches.

It was the birthplace of the Renaissance, Enlightenment, and Industrial Revolution.

Cities

Major cities include Paris, London, Rome, Berlin, and Madrid.

Europe uses many languages, with English, French, and German among the most spoken.

History

It has a deep historical influence, from Ancient Greece to the World Wars. European cuisine varies widely, from Italian pasta to Swedish meatballs.
